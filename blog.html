    Exciting radio update!

    Today after work I was parked outside a truck stop, I was there for about 25 minutes doing the normal "breaker breaker one niner is there anybody out there? " (I got the fuze in my radio fixed btw) And after that 25 minutes of nothing I finally got someone. My conversation with him was very breif but it was my first contact on this radio. I was very excited when I finally heard those words that I was waiting so long to hear. He said "I'm here", I was so happy, I then asked for a mic check and about a minute later he responded with "Loud and clear", I have him a "yeah yeah good buddy so where'ya heading?" and then nothing. I was pretty upset that I lost him but I was very happy that I got someone. So anywho, this has been my blog update, hope you enjoyed, come back soon to take a look at my new posts, I try to post at least twice a week. Thanks for tuneing in!
